Privacy

SoloSphere Events, the Event Booking App, collects personal information from users to facilitate event bookings and provide related services, for the following purposes:

  • making event bookings and reservations
  • providing event-related notifications and updates
  • processing payments and refunds
  • ensuring a personalized user experience
  • improving app features and functionality
  • maintaining security and preventing fraud

SoloSphere Events is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your personal information. We have established practices and procedures to ensure that your personal information is secure, confidential, and used only for the purposes stated above.

SoloSphere Events only discloses user personal information to third parties based on your consent or if required by law. We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Privacy breaches

A privacy breach is when personal information is stolen, lost, or is collected, used, or disclosed without authority.

If you believe a privacy breach has occurred, please contact us immediately at support@solosphereevents.ca to report the incident.

Freedom of Information (FOI)

As an event booking app, SoloSphere Events does not fall under the scope of Freedom of Information legislation.

Privacy policies and procedures

For SoloSphere Events' privacy policies and procedures, please refer to our Privacy Policy section within the app.

Privacy contact information

For inquiries regarding our privacy practices or to report a privacy issue, please contact us at support@solosphereevents.ca.
